About Us

We are The Kindness Collective, a group of eight university students passionate about giving back to the community. We’re partnering with the Devon and Exeter Institution to raise awareness and support for their mission of preserving knowledge and inspiring lifelong learning.

Why Attend?

✨ Unwind and de-stress with a mindful, artistic activity.

✨ Make your own beautiful, handcrafted bookmarks.

✨ Support the Devon and Exeter Institution, a historic educational charity.

✨ Connect with fellow book lovers in a unique, inspiring setting.

✨ Learn about the importance of preserving literature and history.
